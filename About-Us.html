Water & Industry

In a world where water is an increasingly precious commodity, accurate and appropriate treatment of water is ever more complex and imperative. Thanks to a broad, versatile, and in-depth experience built in this sector, MCS has developed specific products for multiple applications in various markets, supporting customers and leaders in the industry. MCS's dosing systems for water treatment include solenoid and motor-driven mechanical pumps, measurement, and control instruments, and a wide range of applications. Pre-collected boards for estimating and checking the synthetic and actual properties of water are just a couple of instances where MCS has shown prowess on the particular necessities of the market and of clients. Fast and Simple. Made possible with 10 years’ worth of engineering. With the ever-increasing need of the market, we always strive to provide our customers with simple installations without the fuss of maintenance. Our products go through rigorous and robust testing mechanisms to ensure that their reliability is ensured for years to come. The design and R&D of metering units with tanks, stirrers, metering pumps, and their accessories, together with systems for automatic polyelectrolyte preparation by our engineers, complete the range of products based solely on an approach keeping customers and clients necessity in mind.

An Ode to Water

Without water, there is no scope for existence. The scarcity of water serves as a vital reminder for us to constantly design better products that have no adverse effect on the environment. Water is becoming scarcer by the day. Since the ’90s, our mission has always been to preserve and minimize water wastage. Since the turn of the century, our team has been working extensively to mitigate the challenges faced by organizations. The range of such challenges is varied, ranging from guaranteeing high water quality to meeting strict norms, which is closely monitored by government regulations and environmental organizations. From the treatment of water for human consumption to its use in cooling treatment and its application over myriad and diverse industrial processes, MCS has and continues to enjoy a strong foothold as the most reliable and consistent player to deliver water technology solutions, tailored to meet any needs whatsoever. Our ‘Ode to Water’ is to bring ‘harmony between human usability with proper utilization of freshwater’. Such a value has helped us in pushing our technological prowess over any of our competitors.
